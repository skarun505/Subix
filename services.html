Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes. We specialize in Series A+ startups and established SMEs looking to scale past their current plateau. However, we also have "Growth System" packages for earlier stage founders ready to invest.

Is it a monthly retainer or project fee?

It depends. System builds (like LeadOS or Custom Apps) are one-time project fees. Performance marketing, content engines, and ongoing optimization are monthly retainers.

What tech stack do you use?

We are platform agnostic but prefer modern stacks: React/Next.js/React Native for apps, HubSpot/Salesforce for CRMs, and Python/Node.js for backend automation.

How fast can you launch?

For standard growth systems, we aim for a 2-week turnaround. Custom software builds typically take 4-8 weeks depending on complexity.

Do you offer white-label services?

Yes, we partner with other agencies to provide back-end development and automation reliability under their brand.

What is your refund policy?

We work on clear milestones. IF we fail to deliver the agreed-upon scope, we offer pro-rated refunds. For performance marketing, ad spend is paid directly to platforms.
