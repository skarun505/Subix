The Command Center for High-Stakes Sales

Subix LeadOS is a high-performance CRM and Lead-to-Revenue Operating System designed for industries like Real Estate, Financial Services, and B2B Sales.

Unlike traditional clunky CRMs, LeadOS combines a stunning dark-themed interface with lightning-fast performance and intelligent automation. It bridges the gap between getting a lead and depositing the check by organizing your entire sales workflow into a single, intuitive dashboard.

Core Capabilities

Visual Pipeline Management

Kanban Mastery: Drag and drop leads through custom sales stages with absolute ease.

  • Instant Insights: Badges for contact info, budget, and high-value status (₹50L+).
  • Health Indicators: Spot "Overdue" or "Inactive" leads before they go cold.

Execution-Ready Quick Actions

Stop switching tabs. Handle every interaction directly from the lead card.

  • One-Click Comms: Call, WhatsApp, or Email directly.
  • Integrated Notes: Activity logs and team notes for every lead.
  • Smart Scheduling: Built-in calendar and automated follow-up reminders.

Intelligent Automation

Let the OS handle the busywork while you focus on the human side of the deal.

  • Auto-Assignment: Route leads based on source or value instantly.
  • Lead Scoring: Proprietary algorithm scores leads (1-100) on behavior.
  • Instant Alerts: WhatsApp notifications for high-priority inquiries immediately.

Revenue-Focused Analytics

Live data that actually helps you make decisions, not just pretty charts.

  • Live KPI Tracking: Conversion rates and revenue in real-time.
  • Projected Income: Visualize future earnings and source-wise breakdowns.
